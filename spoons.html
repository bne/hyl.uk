spoon club

Come and whittle at Rowntree Park

Join us at the story circle on the last Sunday of the month from 10:30am

Experience preferable, but not essential

A supply of green wood will be available, but feel free to bring along a log and your own tools

spoons@hyl.uk

Where?

The Story Circle at Rowntree Park
York, YO23 1JZ

Information on Rowntree Park

Information on parking

When?

The last Sunday of every month from 11am till 2pm

These times may change come summer when we can sit out in the evening, check back here for updates

How much?

£5.00 per person. This covers site hire, wood to carve and some top quality biscuits

Tools

A couple of chopping blocks will be available, but bring your own edge tools

If you're just starting out, to carve a spoon you will need an axe, a straight knife and a curved knife for the bowl

Woodtools do a good starter kit including a Robin Wood axe, alternatively you can sharpen a bog standard hatchet
